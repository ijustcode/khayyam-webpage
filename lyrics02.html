امروز تو را دسترس فردا نیست
واندیشهٔ فردات به جز سودا نیست
ضایع مکن این دم ار دلت شیدا نیست
کاین باقی عمر را بها پیدا نیست

Transliteration of Lyrics to English

Emruz to rā dastras-e fardā nist
Vandishe-ye fardāt be joz sovdā nist
Zāye' makon in dam ar delat sheidā nist
Kin bāqi 'omr rā bahā peydā nist

Literal translation of Lyrics to English

Today, access to tomorrow is denied,
And what you lament for the morrow is merely nervous anticipation.
Do not waste this moment if your heart lacks passion,
Since the value of the remaining lifetime is beyond measure.

Meaning

1. You can't control or guarantee what will happen tomorrow.
2. Worrying about the future is pointless and often just creates unnecessary anxiety.
3. Make the most of the present moment, especially if you feel uninspired or unmotivated.
4. Life is precious and unpredictable, so don't squander the time you have left.

Implications for 21st-century life

This poem reminds people to focus on the present rather than being consumed by anxiety about the future. In a world driven by fast-paced lifestyles and constant planning, it encourages mindfulness, urging individuals to appreciate and make the most of each moment, recognizing the unpredictable nature of life. It emphasizes that true fulfillment comes from living in the now, rather than constantly worrying about what may come.
